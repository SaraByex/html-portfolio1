Best Movies according to Me

My best movies to watch again

1. Pretty Woman

It was a thrilling love story by Richard Gere and Julia Roberts.

2. The Age of Adaline

It was about a beautiful lady who got stuck at a certain age and her looks never changed for a long time which perplexed everyone in her circle.

3. The Intern

It was about a man who got back into the workforce but had to start from entry job as an intern, he performed so well and wowed everyone around him.
